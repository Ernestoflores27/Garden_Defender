void Detector::detect ( )

Method for detecting objects in a frame.

Method for detecting objects in a frame and showing them. Performs non-maximum suppression to eliminate redundant overlapping boxes with lower confidences. Draws predicted bounding boxes on the frame and saves them to the vector.
